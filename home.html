Welcome to accompaniMEnt

In Classical Music monophonic instruments such as violin, cello or flute may need accompaniment. This is called “correpetition” and it is needed especially when monophonic instrument players take place in exams, seminars or concerts. Finding qualified accompanist in such times is vital since s/he can make the sound of the main instrument clearer and can minimize the mistakes of the instrument player. It is hard to find such accompanist considering that there are few pianists comparing to other instrument players and they are hard to reach as they may not share their contact information. Moreover, contacting with the pianist, arranging appointment and asking for appropriate repertoire takes considerable time.

Aim of this system is to connect those customers and accompanists. By this system, every accompanist can design his/her webpage by adding his/her repertoire, appointment schedule, educational background, work experiences, contact information and price while customers can search by filtering city, composer and piece.
